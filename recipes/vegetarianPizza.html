Vegetarian Pizza

Delicious homemade Vegetarian Pizza

    Ingredients
  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Bell peppers (various colors)
  • Red onion
  • Mushrooms
  • Black olives
  • Fresh basil

    Steps
  1. Roll out the pizza dough
  2. Spread tomato sauce over the dough
  3. Layer with cheese and vegetables
  4. Bake in a preheated oven
  5. Remove from the oven, garnish with fresh basil
  6. Slice and enjoy your Vegetarian Pizza!
