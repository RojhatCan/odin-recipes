Chicken Alfredo

Creamy and delicious Chicken Alfredo pasta

    Ingredients
  • Chicken breast
  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Butter
  • Heavy cream
  • Garlic
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper

    Steps
  1. Cook the chicken
  2. Cook the fettuccine pasta
  3. Prepare the Alfredo sauce
  4. Combine chicken, pasta, and sauce
  5. Season to taste with salt and pepper
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
