Lasagna

Finished original italian Lasagna

    Ingredients
  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

    Steps
  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
